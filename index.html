About

  • Techspace

    The purpose of the club is to bring the technology and science community in USICT Campus together into one cohesive club. The Club will work within USICT and reach out to various courses and classes of USICT. The main functions of the club are to encourage academic growth along with technical skills. These functions may consist of, but not limited to, within this intertidal zones, programming contest, robotics contest, cyber law seminars, adaptive digital signal processing, embedded system training, app-­developing concept and training etc. In this way, this Club will cover all the major activities and contests related to major streams and programs offered by USICT.
